OIL (Open Innovation Lab) newsletter rounds-up local open-tech related events every other week. You will see articles and events from both OIL and other open-tech related groups. Seminar: Hands-on HTML5 Hybrid Mobile App with Ionic
2015/11/6 下午 7:30:00 -- 2015/11/6下午 9:00:00
SHB 833, CUHK, Shatin

Open Innovation Lab (OIL) @ CUHK is the hub for advancing the movement of open source, open data, open culture and technology entrepreneurship among engineering students.

Ionic is a HTML5 hybrid mobile app framework which is powered by AngularJS and Cordova. With the comparable appearance and performance, Ionic has reignited the native vs html5 debate and calling itself the world's #1 hybrid mobile SDK. The ionic.io platform has provided the backend services and handles the infrastructure, which makes building, deploying and scaling an app easy.

In the workshop, we will go through the basics of Ionic, try some ionic.io services and of course, get an app up and running.
Organized by Open Innovation Lab @ CUHK

Bao Enrichment Day -- We make DSL bigger, better, stronger!
2015/11/7 下午 3:00:00 --
14F, Kong Ling Building, 100 Jervois Street, Hong Kong

In order to grow the community, work on the lab, contribute to shared projects, to, in general “enrich the Bao“, Saturdays have been officially declared Bao Enrichment Days (B.E.D).As Tuesday’s Hackjam serves as an occasion to show and tell, Saturday’s Bao Enrichment Day is supposed to serve as a day where members and interested outsiders come together to work, not on their personal projects, but on community-oriented stuff. This may range from humble tasks like taking out the trash and giving the space a sweep through, to equipment maintenance, the addition of awesome new gadgets and features, and planning & preparation for workshops and events run by DSL. For too long DimSumLabs has lacked a day of dedicated socialising and making DSL our collective technological home. B.E.D. is here to change this.
