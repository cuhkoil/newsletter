In recent years, Unity3D has become a dominant game development platform for developers around the world. An important factor of its success is accessibility - while being a commercial tool, it is openly accessible to students and beginners who are interested in starting their adventure in the area. Naturally, it is also the platform of choice for many local game developers. Are you interested in the rising industry of game development in Hong Kong?
