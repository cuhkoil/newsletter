Second 3D Hubs Meetup in Hong Kong
02-04-2015 20:00:00 -- 02-04-2015 21:00:00
Dim Sum Labs, 14/F 100 Jervois Street, Sheung Wan, Hong Kong

For the second time, DimsumLabs' 3D Printing Meetup joins forces with 3D Hubs!

The topic of this months MeetUp is the ultra low-cost Infinity 3D Printer, which is currently in development by Marc Rogivue with support of the PolyU. The printer is an excellent ultra low-cost platform ideal for educational purposes. It is assembled by up-cycling of common eWaste materials such as old DVD-Drives, Floppy Drives and PSU's. Through upcycling of eWaste, the printer costs less than USD100!

22-03-2015 15:00:00 -- 22-03-2015 16:30:00
B5-210, AC1, City University of Hong Kong, Kowloon
