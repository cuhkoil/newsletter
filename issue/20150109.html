14-01-2015 14:00:00 -- 14-01-2015 16:00:00

There is a huge problem in the market where Entrepreneurs, Startups and Small Business Owners are having a nightmare while working with developers. On the flip side, developers are having a similar scenario as their communication is not getting through to the clients.
