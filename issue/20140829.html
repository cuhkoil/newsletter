Open Innovation Lab Newsletter

Issue: Aug 29, 2014


OIL (Open Innovation Lab) newsletter rounds-up local open-tech related events every other week. You will see articles and events from both OIL and other open-tech related groups. Join the meetups, workshops and hackathons to embrace open source, open data and open culture. Stay connected with OIL to meet other similar minds and do projects together.

OIL Meetup #3
9/5/2014 20:00:00 -- 9/5/2014 21:00:00
Room 831, Ho Sing-Hang engineering building, The Chinese University of Hong Kong

The 3rd weekly meetup of OIL.

1. Open Source Communities -- Sammy Fung (OSHK)

How is the development of open source communities in Hong Kong, Taiwan and the world ? Sammy will show us pictures of different communities and events, and share his experiences on participating & organizing the communities.

2. A SNS Aggregator App & Bootstrap -- Cheng Wang (4th yr UG @ IE)

During his summer intern, Wang Cheng has implemented a web application to aggregate different social networks and present a unified timeline for the user. This App is based on various open source projects. In this talk, he will demo the final result and walk you through invovled open source projects. In the last part, he will give an introductory excercise of Bootstrap, to show you how to rapidly make beautiful interfaces.
Organized by OIL
