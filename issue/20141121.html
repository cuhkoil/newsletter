一個資深吃貨的港深美食雙城記
23-11-2014 15:00:00 -- 23-11-2014 16:30:00
Academic Building 1 五樓B5-211, 香港城市大學, Kowloon

如今香港深圳兩座都市之間南來北往越來越頻繁，形成了一小時生活圈，所帶來的並非只有《一路向西》的便利，還造就了眾多遊走於兩地的美食達人。深圳人下班可以趕到沙田的街市大排檔吃晚飯，香港人可以在春風路的燒烤攤食完宵夜再過關。然而細究之下，一河之隔的港深兩地卻又有著截然不同的飲食氛圍和流派。本期分享會，將由陳晨分享多年來的吃貨心得，與大家一起探討這麼近那麼遠的港深飲食文化。 本次活動由香港城市大學研究生會讀書俱樂部(CUPA Reading Club)舉辦。
Organized by CUPA ReadingClub

Space and Time in Interstellar Movie（《星际启示录》里的时间和空­间）
26-11-2014 18:30:00 -- 26-11-2014 20:30:00
LT-18，AC-1, City University of Hong Kong, Kowloon

Have you watched the popular movie "Interstellar" recently? Are you confused about the fifth dimension world in the movie? Come to CityU and discuss with a professional physicist!

Professor Ng will first talk about his understanding of science fiction in western culture. Then he will introduce our present understanding of "Space and Time" in physics, and the boundary between "known" and "theoretical speculation" (general relativity - black hole, wormhole; quantum gravity; global warming) and how these elements are cleverly presented in Interstellar.
