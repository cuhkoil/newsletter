Curiosity 3D Printer Building Workshop for kids
2015-10-17 上午10:00:00 -- 2015-10-17 上午12:00:00
16 sze shan street, Yau Tong, Hong Kong

Curiosity 3D Printer Building Class for the curious kids!

What are we building?

A 3D printer using electronic waste that will otherwise go to landfill, each participants will build one 3D printer and they can take the 3D printer home at the end of the workshop.
This printer is easy to build and requires almost no calibration, you can take it home and plug it to your computer and print!
We will be using electronic waste to build part of the printer, through this workshop kids will appreciate the importance of waste reuse and upcycle, and learn useful assembly skills.
We want to emphasize that this is not a fully-fledge 3D printer that you will find off the shelf in the market, it's functionality is limited by it's size, the speed of motors and the limited torque of the motors, however, as you can see from the video, it does have all the basic 3D printing function and is good for printing small items. It is with no doubt an excellent educational tool and ideal for young makers who are new to 3D printing.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_ADmDbiKnlU

When?

We will be hosting the workshop over two Saturdays:
- 17 and 24 October (Saturday)
- 10am - 4pm (including one hour lunch break, you are recommended to bring pack lunch with you. The closest place where you can find food is about 5-10mins walk from MakerBay at Domain Mall)
